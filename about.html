Built on Success

The Jaxx Liberty beta builds on the success of Jaxx, one of the most popular cryptocurrency wallets, downloaded by millions. The new interface introduces a unified dashboard and module-based approach to provide information that everyday people need to navigate the cryptocurrency ecosystem through features including news, market data, portfolio and a multi-chain Block Explorer.

Decentral

The Jaxx wallet software is developed by Decentral Inc., Canada's leading blockchain company. Our founder created Jaxx in 2014 because he saw a change like Netscape for the Internet: that we are moving towards a world of value movement and that people need an easy way to manage their cryptocurrencies and digital lives.

Our office hours are 9am to 5pm EST. You can reach our customer support team 24 hours a day.
