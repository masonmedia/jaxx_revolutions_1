Your Security is Our Mission.

Packed with new security features, Jaxx Liberty is the most secure cryptocurrenty wallet available.

More than a million users already trust Jaxx.

We take security seriously. From industry-standard encryption to key privacy and rigorous code review, our highest priority is ensuring the safety and privacy of our users. Explore some of our new and improved security features below.

Private Keys

Like the legacy version of Jaxx wallet, the user’s secrets (mnemonic/private keys) are stored on the client side instead of the server side, therefore they are not exposed to anyone, even Decentral employees.

Encryption

As soon as a user sets a password, the secrets are encrypted (i.e. “it is encrypted”) and stored securely using industry-standard encryption procedure. They can only be decrypted with the password known by the user. Liberty does not store the user-password in the system.

Address Integrity

Wallet generated addresses are periodically checked in terms of integrity to reduce risk of malicious modification of addresses.

Code Review

Rigid code review processes minimizes security vulnerabilities.

Ownership & Control

Jaxx uses a 12-word “Masterseed” to help you manage the private keys to your Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other blockchain assets.

Masterseed

Use your Masterseed to view your wallet on iOS, Android, Chrome Extension, Windows, Mac and Linux desktops.

Jaxx never has access to your funds.

They remain securely in your control on their respective blockchains. This means that if you back-up your Masterseed you will be easily able to recover your wallets if you lose access to the device they were on.

No personal information required.

Users are not required to provide personal information to interact with their digital assets. We care about safeguarding your digital assets and protecting your identity.
